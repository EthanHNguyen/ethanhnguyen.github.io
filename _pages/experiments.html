Small public experiments, interfaces, and tools. Some are polished artifacts; some are narrower probes into workflows, data, and model behavior.

Uncle Sam’s Cart

A source-backed SAM.gov receipt for the weird stuff Uncle Sam is shopping for.

Retirement Savings Calculator

A small interactive model for testing contribution, employer match, Social Security, and projected income assumptions.

Model Mind Casino

A small game about prediction, probability, and model behavior.

UFO Evidence Review

A clean interface for reviewing official U.S. government UAP records.
