RFP Map

Mobile-first market intelligence radar for SAM.gov opportunities. RFP Map turns federal contract opportunities into a spatial browsing interface: explore agencies, themes, opportunity clusters, and source-linked contract pages without needing to understand procurement search syntax.

  • Live: ethanhn.com/rfp-map/
  • Source: github.com/EthanHNguyen/rfp-map
  • Stack: Next.js, Tailwind, static SAM.gov data export, GitHub Pages
  • Design principle: curiosity-first public browser for open contracts, not capture triage software
  • What it proves: messy public data can become an exploratory interface, not just another search box

Technical signal pipeline

A daily ML engineering intelligence workflow that filters X/Twitter, Hacker News, and web sources into a concise technical signal report. The goal is not news consumption. It is taste calibration: model releases, infra shifts, security incidents, benchmarks, and debunks that matter to builders.

  • Focus: frontier models, agentic coding, AI infra, security, evaluation, and geopolitics
  • What it proves: high-signal technical synthesis from noisy streams
