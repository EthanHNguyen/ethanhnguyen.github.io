Overview Evidence Findings Method

The short version

We found curiosity, not proof of anomalous craft.

The recovered evidence does not support a conclusion of anomalous craft, exotic technology, or extraordinary performance. That is not the same as every file being boring. It means the public evidence is usually missing the data needed to make strong physical claims.

  • Recovered videos: the three measured priority videos are currently best classified as likely explained.
  • Still images: redactions and missing sequences keep many image cases unresolved due to missing data.
  • PDFs: most documents are context records, not direct observations.
  • Current anomalous residual count: 0.

Why so cautious?

Strong UAP claims need exact time, exact location, range, platform motion, sensor calibration, and unredacted frame sequences. Most public records do not provide that package.
